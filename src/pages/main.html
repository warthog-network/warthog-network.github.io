WHAT IS WARTHOG?

Warthog is an experimental cryptocurrency with world's first Proof of Balanced Work algorithm written by a group of crypto enthusiasts. It is meant to be a community effort and everyone is more than welcome to join and contribute. Who knows how big this project will be?

TOKENOMICS

This is not the default kind of project where you see a disproportionately high allocation of the total supply unfairly reserved for the team, the advisors and their pockets. 0% is minted, premined, reserved for anyone, 100% is publicly mineable.
